2 ‚Äî Health & Wellness Center

Files: Pranav/case2/case2.cpp ‚Ä¢ Pranav/case2/case2.csv

The Health & Wellness Center offers preventive and immediate healthcare services, combining a gym, physiotherapy and basic diagnostics. It reduces travel time for students and residents seeking care, and supports a healthier campus community. Accessible healthcare facilities are essential for resilient urban neighborhoods.

Aligned SDGs: SDG 3 (Good Health & Well-being), SDG 11 (Sustainable Cities)
Data structure: Min-heap (priority_queue)
Algorithm: Priority scheduling simulation
  1. Load appointments with arrival time, priority and service time.
  2. Sort by arrival time; push arrivals into min-heap keyed by priority (then arrival).
  3. Simulate multiple servers: pick top-priority patient when server free, schedule, update server availability.
  4. Compute avg wait and throughput.
  5. Report top-wait patients and server utilization.

3 ‚Äî Eco-Friendly Food Court

Files: Pranav/case3/case3.cpp ‚Ä¢ Pranav/case3/case3.csv

The Eco-Friendly Food Court offers healthy, low-waste dining options and uses sustainable packaging to reduce environmental impact. It can partner with local suppliers to minimize transport emissions and provide nutritious food near hostels and campus facilities. Discounting soon-to-expire food reduces waste and supports food security.

Aligned SDGs: SDG 2 (Zero Hunger), SDG 12 (Responsible Consumption)
Data structure: Bucket queues + min-heap
Algorithm: Earliest-expiry first with discounting
  1. Load items with expiry days, price and other details.
  2. Bucket items by expiry days; maintain a min-heap for earliest expiry items.
  3. When serving, pop earliest-expiry; apply discount policy if expiry low.
  4. Track revenue, discounts and donate if near-zero expiry.
  5. Output expiry distribution and served summary.

4 ‚Äî EV Charging Station

Files: Pranav/case4/case4.cpp ‚Ä¢ Pranav/case4/case4.csv

The EV Charging Station provides fast and reliable charging for electric vehicles and supports the city's clean mobility goals. A nearby convenience store increases convenience for users while vehicles charge. Smart routing to the least-wait station makes for efficient charging and lower emissions.

Aligned SDGs: SDG 7 (Affordable & Clean Energy), SDG 13 (Climate Action)
Data structure: Graph (adjacency list) + Dijkstra
Algorithm: Shortest-path scoring
  1. Load requests and station info from CSV.
  2. Build adjacency list for nearby roads/stations.
  3. Run Dijkstra from request origin to candidate stations to get travel times.
  4. Compute score = travel_time + normalized_wait; pick min score.
  5. Output chosen station and score distribution.

Aligned SDGs: SDG 9 (Industry, Innovation & Infrastructure), SDG 12 (Responsible Consumption)

Aligned SDGs: SDG 11 (Sustainable Cities), SDG 7 (Clean Energy)

9 ‚Äî Skill Development & Coaching Center

Files: Pranav/case9/case9.cpp ‚Ä¢ Pranav/case9/case9.csv

The Skill Development Center offers courses and coaching to improve employability and technical skills among students. Structured timetabling and conflict-free slot assignment make the center scalable and accessible. Partnerships with local employers create internship pipelines for trainees.

Aligned SDGs: SDG 4 (Quality Education), SDG 8 (Decent Work)
Data structure: Adjacency matrix + greedy coloring (DSATUR-like)
Algorithm: Graph coloring for slot assignment
  1. Build course conflict graph from student registrations.
  2. Use DSATUR-like greedy ordering: pick vertex with highest saturation.
  3. Assign smallest available color (time-slot) not used by neighbors.
  4. Repeat until all courses colored; minimize colors used.
  5. Output mapping course -> time-slot and number of slots used.

10 ‚Äî Entertainment & Gaming Zone

Files: Pranav/case10/case10.cpp ‚Ä¢ Pranav/case10/case10.csv

The Entertainment & Gaming Zone offers safe, modern recreational facilities including VR and arcade areas. It supports mental well-being and community interaction for students and families. Smart queueing creates a fair and low-wait experience for both VIP and regular visitors.

Aligned SDGs: SDG 3 (Well-being), SDG 11 (Sustainable Cities)
Data structure: Deque (std::deque) with ageing checks
Algorithm: FCFS with VIP front insertion + ageing promotion
  1. Load session requests with type (vip/regular), arrival and duration.
  2. Enqueue VIPs to front, regulars to back.
  3. Before serving, if a regular has waited > THRESHOLD, promote to front (ageing).
  4. Serve from front, update current time, accumulate wait stats.
  5. Report served count and average wait.
