ğŸ—ï¸ Rajdwar â€“ City Design Project

City design is a complex and multi-dimensional challenge that demands careful planning, optimization, and sustainability. The City Design Project â€“ Rajdwar uses algorithmic thinking and DAA concepts to plan roads, services, business zones, and public utilities for a smart, balanced city.

We model the city as a graph, where intersections and key structures become nodes and roads become edges. Using shortest path algorithms, greedy strategies, and optimization techniques, we look at how travel time, connectivity, and access to essential services can be improved.

The project focuses on:

  • Minimizing travel distance and congestion using graph algorithms.
  • Planning public transport, residential areas, and business clusters more intelligently.
  • Ensuring quick access to hospitals, education, and emergency services.
  • Aligning growth with sustainability and SDG-11: Sustainable Cities and Communities.
