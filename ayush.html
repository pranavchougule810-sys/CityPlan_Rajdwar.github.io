Urban Business Structures & SDG 11 Contributions

Prepared by Ayush Kumar Ojha – Development of a Sustainable City Project

Information Technology (IT Sector)

SDG Target 11.3 – Sustainable Urban Planning
  • Developing smart-city solutions such as traffic systems, waste tracking platforms, and environmental monitoring tools.
  • Strengthening the city's knowledge-based economy and creating skilled employment opportunities.
  • Supporting sustainable infrastructure through digital twins, GIS mapping, and energy-efficient building systems.

Hotel Management

SDG Target 11.3 – Sustainable Urban Development
  • Providing safe and hygienic accommodation that supports structured urban growth.
  • Reducing congestion by being located near major transport routes and commercial districts.
  • Promoting sustainability through water conservation, waste reduction, and energy-efficient practices.

Bar Management

SDG Target 11.7 – Safe & Inclusive Public Spaces
  • Creating safe and controlled social environments that reduce illegal or unsafe drinking areas.
  • Contributing to urban vibrancy through regulated nightlife zones with proper security.
  • Implementing sustainable practices such as waste segregation and noise-reduction systems.

Post Office Management

SDG Target 11.1 – Access to Basic Services
  • Providing essential communication and delivery services for households, businesses, and government offices.
  • Supporting digital and financial inclusion for vulnerable and low-income groups.
  • Strengthening organized and secure parcel logistics within the city.

Petrol Pump Management

SDG Target 11.6 – Reducing Environmental Impact
  • Providing safe and regulated fueling services, reducing reliance on informal and unsafe fuel sources.
  • Supporting green mobility through EV charging and CNG refueling infrastructure.
  • Reducing emissions with vapour-recovery systems and modern pollution control equipment.

Supermarket Management

SDG Target 11.1 – Essential Urban Services
  • Offering accessible and affordable food and essentials to residents in a clean environment.
  • Reducing street congestion by organizing retail into structured commercial spaces.
  • Minimizing environmental impact through waste management and recyclable packaging.

Vehicle Showroom Management

SDG Target 11.2 – Sustainable Transport Systems
  • Encouraging adoption of low-emission and electric vehicles.
  • Providing regulated service centers that prevent pollution from informal workshops.
  • Improving sustainable mobility through EV charging and awareness initiatives.

Hospital Management

SDG Target 11.3 – Accessible Urban Services
  • Providing essential medical care to ensure public health and urban safety.
  • Enhancing emergency preparedness and disaster response capabilities.
  • Ensuring safe biomedical waste management to protect environmental health.

School Management

SDG Target 11.7 – Inclusive Public Spaces
  • Offering accessible education within planned neighborhoods to reduce long travel distances.
  • Providing safe learning environments that support social and cultural development.
  • Maintaining playgrounds and community halls that serve as inclusive public spaces.

Market (Bazaar) Management

SDG Target 11.6 – Reducing Environmental Impact
  • Organizing commercial activity into clean, structured spaces that reduce congestion.
  • Supporting local vendors through regulated, hygienic retail environments.
  • Improving cleanliness using proper waste disposal, sanitation, and drainage systems.
