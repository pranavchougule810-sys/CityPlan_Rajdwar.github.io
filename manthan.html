City Infrastructure Components & Their Impact

1. Mall

  • Supports planned urban development by consolidating commerce, services, and public spaces into a single organized hub, reducing urban sprawl.
  • Strengthens the local economy by generating formal employment and integrating MSMEs and informal businesses into a regulated ecosystem.
  • Enhances quality of urban life through safe, accessible spaces for shopping, social interaction, and recreation.
  • Reduces environmental load via efficient land use, controlled traffic movement, and centralized waste and resource management.
  • Promotes safety, resilience, and inclusivity through professional governance, emergency systems, and barrier-free access.

2. Movie Theatre

  • Supports planned urban development by centralizing entertainment in a regulated and organized environment.
  • Generates formal employment in operations, security, food services, and technical roles, supporting inclusive economic growth (SDG 8).
  • Enhances cultural and educational access through films, documentaries, community screenings, and regional cinema (SDG 11.7, SDG 4.7).
  • Improves urban quality of life by offering safe, affordable, and accessible entertainment for all age groups.
  • Promotes social inclusion through barrier-free access, discounted shows, and community-based cultural events.

3. Hotels

  • Supports planned urban development by providing organized accommodation infrastructure within commercial and mixed-use zones.
  • Generates formal employment in hospitality, housekeeping, food services, maintenance, and administration (SDG 8).
  • Strengthens tourism, business travel, and conference ecosystems within the city (SDG 11).
  • Enhances urban liveability by offering safe, regulated, and accessible lodging facilities.
  • Promotes skill development and learning through hospitality training, internships, and service-sector education (SDG 4).

4. Hospital

  • Strengthens urban healthcare infrastructure by providing organized and accessible medical services (SDG 11).
  • Improves public health outcomes through diagnosis, treatment, emergency care, and preventive services (SDG 3).
  • Generates large-scale formal employment for healthcare professionals and support staff (SDG 8).
  • Enhances health education through medical training, internships, and awareness programs (SDG 4).
  • Promotes social inclusion by offering affordable and emergency healthcare services.

5. Spa

  • Promotes physical and mental well-being through stress relief, therapy, and wellness services (SDG 3).
  • Generates formal employment in wellness, therapy, and hospitality sectors (SDG 8).
  • Enhances urban quality of life by integrating preventive health and relaxation facilities within city spaces (SDG 11).

6. ATM Facilities (Including Additional Locations)

  • Improves access to essential financial services such as cash withdrawal and basic banking (SDG 9).
  • Reduces travel time and congestion by decentralizing banking infrastructure within neighborhoods (SDG 11).
  • Enhances redundancy and convenience by placing ATMs in high-footfall zones such as malls, transit hubs, and residential clusters.
  • Supports daily economic activities for residents, workers, and local businesses.

7. Parking Facilities

  • Reduces roadside congestion by providing organized and designated vehicle storage (SDG 11).
  • Improves traffic flow, pedestrian safety, and emergency vehicle access.
  • Supports last-mile connectivity in dense urban areas (SDG 9).

8. Pharmacy

  • Ensures timely access to medicines, medical supplies, and basic healthcare needs (SDG 3).
  • Supports public health resilience during emergencies and routine medical requirements.
  • Enhances healthcare accessibility within residential and mixed-use urban zones (SDG 11).

9. Food and Pharma Delivery Services

  • Improves last-mile access to food and medicines for residents, elderly individuals, and patients.
  • Generates gig-based and formal employment in logistics and delivery services (SDG 8).
  • Reduces crowding in public spaces by enabling efficient doorstep delivery solutions (SDG 11).

10. Grocery Store

  • Ensures daily food security and access to essential household supplies (SDG 2).
  • Reduces dependency on long-distance travel for basic daily needs.
  • Supports neighborhood-level economic activity and local employment (SDG 11).

11. Delivery Centre

  • Strengthens urban logistics and supply-chain efficiency (SDG 9).
  • Generates employment in warehousing, sorting, and transportation sectors (SDG 8).
  • Supports the growth of e-commerce and doorstep service delivery in cities (SDG 11).

12. Parks and Open Spaces

  • Provides accessible public open spaces for recreation, relaxation, and social interaction (SDG 11.7).
  • Improves physical and mental health through green environments and active lifestyles (SDG 3).
  • Enhances urban livability, environmental balance, and climate resilience.
